Pandemic

The goal of game is to eliminate all infections from the board.

New infections will hit random cities every 4 seconds.

Click on a city to remove one infection.

Below the board are multiple choice questions about Javascript. Every 3 answers you get correct will give you the ability to 'quarantine' a city. This allows you to prevent any infections from hitting that city.

Once you answer 6 questions correctly, you will remove 2 infections per click.
